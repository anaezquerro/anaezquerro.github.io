Hi👋 I am Ana Ezquerro

I’m an NLP researcher at the LyS Group (University of A Coruña). I previously obtained a bachelor’s degree in Data Science and Engineering (July 2023), and, recently, a master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence (February 2025).

My initial research covers time series analysis and machine learning algorithms applied to statistics. Right now I am working on graph/tree (incremental) parsing, multimodal learning and efficient and human-like language representations.
