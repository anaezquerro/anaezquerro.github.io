Hi👋 I am Ana Ezquerro.

I’m a second-year student in the Master in Artificial Intelligence at University of A Coruña and a full-time researcher at the LyS Group. I previously obtained my bachelor degree in Data Science and Engineering in 2023.

My initial research covers time series analysis and machine learning algorithms applied to statistics and right now I am working on graph/tree (incremental) parsing and multimodal learning. I am also planning a PhD degree on human-like incremental language representations.
