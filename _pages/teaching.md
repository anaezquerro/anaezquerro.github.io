### **Supervision**
During my research time at the [LyS Group](https://www.grupolys.org/lys_en.html) I had the opportunity to co-supervise the following bachelor theses at the [University of A Coruña](https://www.udc.es), all of them scored with >8.5 out of 10 points and some of them highlighted with honor distinctions. 

- *[Development of a database for the analysis of housing rentals](https://ruc.udc.es/entities/publication/2870e5a2-5310-42f3-978d-3b346d33fb5d)* by [Antía López Rodríguez](mailto:antia.lopezr@udc.es), co-supervised with [Ángeles Saavedra Places](https://pdi.udc.es/en/File/Pdi/PL7AF).
- 
- *Modeling and linguistic analysis of speeches in the Congress using NLP* (to appear) by [Inés González Rodríguez](ines.gonzalez.rodriguez@udc.es), co-supervised with [David Vilares](https://www.grupolys.org/~david.vilares/).

- *Training dynamics for the characterization of synthetic data in NLP* (to appear) by [Irene Lago Pato](i.lago@udc.es), co-supervised with [David Vilares](https://www.grupolys.org/~david.vilares/).


Although I am now pursuing my PhD at TU Graz, I remain open to co-supervise theses at both institutions. If you are a student interested in collaboration on any of the following topics feel free to reach out! I am open to hear new projects from students, as well as propose challenging problems to present as bachelor's and master's theses.

- Abstract Visual Reasoning.
- Diffusion Models for Reasoning Problems.
- Efficient Generative Modeling.
- Syntactic and semantic parsing.
- Graph learning and applications.
